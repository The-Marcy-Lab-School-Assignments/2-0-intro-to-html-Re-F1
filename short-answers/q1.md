# Question
What's the difference between "semantic" and "non-semantic" elements?

# Answer
**Semantic**
> h tags are headings
>
> a are links
>
> p are paragraphs

**Non-semantic**
>div
>
>span
>
>These are just for grouping other related tags together (div) or calling out a particular section of text (span).
